PROGRAMS

Here are the various programs offered by different universities under the Rajasthan Technical Education Department.

ENGINEERING

The Department of Technical Education (DTE) Rajasthan offers a variety of engineering courses across polytechnic and engineering institutes, focusing on disciplines like Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Computer Science. These programs combine theory and practical skills to prepare students for successful careers in the engineering field.

POLYTECHNIC

The Department of Technical Education (DTE) Rajasthan offers polytechnic courses that focus on practical skills in fields like Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical Engineering. These courses are designed to provide hands-on training, preparing students for technical roles in various industries.

DIPLOMA

DTE Rajasthan's diploma courses cover a range of technical and vocational disciplines, offering students specialized knowledge in areas such as Electronics, Computer Science, and Automotive Engineering. These programs are aimed at equipping students with the skills needed for immediate employment or further studies.
